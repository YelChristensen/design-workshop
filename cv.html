It was the nerve system of the largest empire in human history. Genghis Khan’s mighty horse messenger system connected ½ the planet. You square up to 1000km of Mongolian steppe on semi-wild horses, changing steed every 40km. You navigate and survive on your own wits and skill, living among the herders. It’s you and your horse vs the wild. This is the longest and toughest horse race on earth. This is the Mongol Derby.
